⚠️ Before You BookAntes de Reservar

A scheduling fee is required at the time of booking to secure your appointment. This fee is non-refundable and will be applied toward your total balance. Remaining balance is paid on the day of your appointment. Se requiere un cargo de reserva al momento de agendar para confirmar tu cita. Este cargo no es reembolsable y se aplicará a tu balance total. Appointments must be cancelled or rescheduled at least 24 hours in advance. No-shows forfeit the scheduling fee and may be required to prepay future appointments in full. Las citas deben cancelarse o reprogramarse con al menos 24 horas de anticipación. La inasistencia sin aviso pierde el cargo de reserva y puede requerir pago completo en futuras citas.
